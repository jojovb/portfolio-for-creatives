"You Deserve Love" Ad Campaign

Probably the first advertising campaign I ever worked on, “You Deserve Love” is an unofficial campaign for The Trevor Project; an organization that provides educational and non-urgent services for LGBTQ+ youth and allies. The goal with this campaign was to push for more social media presence to be more easily accessible to young people.
