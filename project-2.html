Dreamscape Music Festival

With the rising popularity of K-pop in North America, I decided to create the visual identity for Dreamscape, a fictitious music festival that celebrates the generations of women in K-pop. I was interested in experimenting with 3D graphics for the first time, and settled on the imagery of a strawberry in the sky; something that communicates a dream-like quality.
