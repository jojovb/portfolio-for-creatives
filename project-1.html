Personal Brand Logo

I wanted my personal logo to encapsulate my friendly, light-hearted personality while still keeping things relatively clean and minimal. I am a lover of all things cute and cartoon-y, so when I came up with an icon of a little black cat and speech bubble in the shape of the letter J (the initial of my first name) I knew I had to go for it. I often feel like my personality embodies that of a cat; I am naturally independent, but also have a playful/social side that comes out when the mood strikes (not to mention my absolute LOVE for naps).
